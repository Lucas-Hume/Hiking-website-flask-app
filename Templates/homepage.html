Who we are

Hello everyone.

This is ICC Sydney's Hobo Robo Club — a group of friends inspired by social media to start hiking together.

Lucas initiated the idea after forming a deeper friendship with Shri, Leon, and Ellaine.

How it all began

In late May 2024, the original group completed the Overcliff–Undercliff Track in Wentworth Falls.

From that day, we committed to continuing these hikes, with Lucas acting as the planner under a benevolent dictatorship disguised as democracy.

Why we continued

Mutual suffering and the chance to escape Sydney brought us together. We now share completed trails, photos, difficulty ratings, and upcoming adventures.

So please have a geez and look at what we have accomplished and what our next trail will be!
